Traditional VS Digital

Traditional 2D animation creates movement frame-by-frame, primarily by hand-drawing on paper or transparent cels, while digital 2D animation uses computer software and tablets offering greater speed and flexibility.

Frame-By-Frame

Frame-by-frame line art 2D animation is a traditional method where each frame of a scene is individually drawn to create the illusion of movement when played in sequence.

Line Art Motion

Creates the illusion of movement in a flat space by sequencing slightly different drawings rapidly to tell stories with stylized visuals.
